# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, level of experience, or role.

Examples of expected behavior:

- giving and receiving constructive feedback
- assuming good faith while staying technically rigorous
- respecting different viewpoints and lived experiences
- focusing criticism on ideas, code, and behavior rather than people

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- sustained disruption of discussion or review processes

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues,
and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
repository maintainers or administrators. Public callouts should be avoided when
private moderation is possible.

## Consequences

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings,
temporary bans, permanent bans, or removal of content.
